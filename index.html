Welcome to Our Air Pollution Awareness Project

Air pollution is a significant environmental issue that affects the health of millions worldwide. This project aims to raise awareness about the causes, effects, and solutions to air pollution, especially in rural areas like Swayambupuram village.

Through surveys and research, we have gathered valuable insights into the impacts of air pollution on health, agriculture, and the environment. Our findings highlight the urgent need for action and sustainable practices.
